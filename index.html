About NanoBiz

NanoBiz is a mobile application designed to help small and micro businesses grow and gain better visibility. Many small businesses struggle to reach customers on platforms like Instagram or other social media platforms. NanoBiz provides a dedicated platform where businesses can showcase their services, connect with customers, and expand their reach in their local communities.

Platform Features

🔍 Local Business Discovery

Search and explore businesses city-wise to easily find services near you.

💬 Direct Chat

Customers can directly communicate with business owners for inquiries and bookings.

📊 Business Analytics

Business owners get insights and analytics to understand growth and engagement.

🚀 Subscription Plans

Premium plans help businesses improve their visibility and reach more customers.

🌍 Dedicated Platform

A marketplace focused entirely on empowering small and micro businesses.

Development Status

NanoBiz is currently in the development phase and new features are being actively built. The platform aims to launch soon with tools that support small businesses in growing their online presence and connecting with local customers.
