About NanoBiz

NanoBiz is a mobile application designed to help small and micro businesses grow and get better visibility. Many small businesses struggle to reach customers on platforms like Instagram or other social media. NanoBiz provides a dedicated platform where businesses can showcase their services and connect with potential customers.

Features

  • Search and discover local businesses city-wise
  • Direct chat between customers and business owners
  • Business analytics to track growth and engagement
  • Subscription plans to improve business visibility
  • Dedicated platform for small and micro businesses

Current Status

The NanoBiz application is currently in the development phase. More features and improvements will be released soon.
