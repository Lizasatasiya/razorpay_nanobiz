About NanoBiz

NanoBiz is a modern platform built to support small and micro businesses that struggle to gain visibility on crowded platforms like Instagram. It provides a focused ecosystem where businesses can showcase their services, connect directly with customers, and grow sustainably.

Platform Features

🔍 Local Discovery

Find businesses nearby with smart city-based search and filtering.

💬 Direct Communication

Chat instantly with business owners for quick inquiries and bookings.

📊 Insights & Analytics

Track performance, visibility, and customer engagement.

🚀 Growth Plans

Boost reach and visibility with premium subscription plans.

🌍 Dedicated Marketplace

A platform built specifically for micro and local businesses.
